The Relationship between Delaunay Triangulations, Voronoi diagrams, and 3D Convex Hulls

One way to compute the Voronoi diagram of a pointset in O(nlogn) is to lift the points to a paraboloid and compute the convex hull. The paraboloid is of the form x2 + y2 z2, so that its x-y cross sections are circles.
